Managing your videos An easy way to keep track of videos on your computer is to use the Videos library. By default, the Videos library shows all the videos located in the My Videos folder, but you can include other folders in your Videos library, too. For more information, see Include folders in a library. Note To watch videos in Windows, you need a program designed for playing video files. If your computer didn’t come with such a program, you'll need to install one before you can watch videos on your computer.
